Setting Up the 3CX App on Your Phone

Follow the steps below to install or reinstall the 3CX app using your QR code.

📱  iPhone & Android
Select Your Situation
1
Install the 3CX app
1
Download and install the 3CX app for your device:
 iPhone — iOS App Store ▶ Android — Google Play
2
Open the app. Read and accept the License Agreement when it appears.
3
When prompted for permissions, tap Allow for both Camera and Microphone. These are required for the app to work.
2
Configure with your QR code
4
Check the reply we sent to your support ticket — your personal QR code is attached to that message.
5
Scan the QR code using your phone’s camera app, or use the scanner inside the 3CX app.
⚠️
Do not manually type your extension number or server address. Always scan the QR code we provide — typing this information is the most common cause of blank screens and silent failures.
6
The app will configure itself automatically. Once complete, your extension will show as Registered and you can make and receive calls.
ℹ️
QR code is single-use. If the scan fails or the code appears expired, reply to your support ticket and we will send a fresh one right away.
ℹ️
Calls not connecting, blank screen, or login errors? Follow these steps in order — a clean reinstall resolves most issues.
1
Remove the existing app
1
Delete the 3CX app completely from your phone.
iPhone: Press and hold the app icon → Remove App → Delete App.
Android: Press and hold the app icon → Uninstall.
2
Restart your phone — fully power it off, then back on. This clears cached configuration that causes silent failures.
2
Reinstall and configure
3
Reinstall the 3CX app for your device:
 iPhone — iOS App Store ▶ Android — Google Play
4
Open the app. Read and accept the License Agreement, then allow Camera and Microphone permissions.
5
Scan the QR code we attached to your support ticket reply using your phone’s camera or the 3CX app scanner.
⚠️
Do not manually type your extension number or server address. Always scan the QR code we provide — typing this information is the most common cause of blank screens and silent failures.
6
Your extension will register automatically. Test by placing a call or asking someone to call your extension.
ℹ️
QR code is single-use. If the code has already been scanned or has expired, reply to your support ticket and we will generate a new one.
